Real options capture the value of adapting decisions as new information is revealed. They are critical in high-uncertainty projects like AI & Cloud.

Valor estratégico de la opción de esperar: Aunque en este caso invertir hoy es superior, la opción de esperar crea valor cuando hay alta incertidumbre y el downside es severo. La opción elimina el downside (escenario bajo) al permitir no invertir si las condiciones son desfavorables — transformando la distribución de resultados de simétrica a asimétrica positiva. Strategic value of the delay option: Although investing today is superior in this case, the delay option creates value when uncertainty is high and the downside is severe. The option eliminates the downside (low state) by allowing the firm not to invest if conditions are unfavorable — transforming the outcome distribution from symmetric to positively asymmetric.
